Guys looking for buddies to hang with!

bromanceconnection.com is an online service to bring together guys with like interests. You can find guys in your geographic area to do fun (whatever you consider to be fun) things together.

About bromanceconnection.com

There are so many online dating sites for singles to meet other singles to start a romantic relationship. But what about men looking for other men friends. bromanceconnection.com is geared for men of any age looking for guy friends. Maybe you want to find some golf buddies, someone to shoot pool with, a friend to meet for a beer to watch a football game with…

To join bromanceconnection.com, simply create a profile and provide some background info about yourself, as well as describe what kind of new friends you’re interested in meeting. Once you’ve created your profile, you will be able to browse our network to find your connections. You may select connections by any criteria you choose – age, location, hobbies, etc.

Ideally, you’re on your way to planning an outing with your new buddies.

It’s time to join bromanceconnection.com!

This service was founded by Jim Anan & Kim Ciraulo in 2013. The need was discovered by surveying women who found that, due to divorce or widowhood, they had no “single girlfriends” with similar hobbies or who were open to new hobbies or who just wanted to expand their social networks.
