  • women looking for new girlfriend connections!

    Girlfriendsconnection.com is an online service to bring together women with like interests. It is designed to enhance your free time by forming relationships with similar women. You can connect with other women in your geographic area to do fun (whatever you consider to be fun) things together.

    Perhaps you’d like a workout or walking buddy or girlfriends who enjoy going out dancing (in hopes of meeting single, potential dates). If you’re looking for new girlfriends to socialize with, sign up for girlfriendsconnection.com!

  • why join girlfriendsconnection.com?

    girlfriendsconnection.com is designed specifically to help you connect with new friends. To join girlfriendsconnection.com, simply create a profile and provide some background info about yourself, as well as describe what kind of new friends you’re interested in meeting.

    Once you’ve created your profile, you will be able to browse our network to find your girlfriend connections. You may select matches by any criteria you choose – age, location, hobbies, etc.

    You will then be able to get in touch with that new potential girlfriend(s) through girlfriendsconnection.com’s website. Ideally, you’re on your way to planning an outing with your new girlfriend(s).

    Sign up for girlfriendsconnection.com today, meet new friends and plan an exciting GNO (girls night out)!

looking to connect with new women?

Many women in their 30s, 40s, 50s & beyond, have core friends that they hang out with which is great. But what about on the weekend when those female friends are with their husbands or partners? Are you tired of being a 3rd or 5th wheel to your friends who are part of a couple? Would you like to meet new women who are at the same stage in life as you? Wouldn’t it be nice to have girlfriends who want to go to the movies, vacation or out dancing?

It’s time to join girlfriendsconnection.com!

about us

Girlfriendsconnection was founded by Kim Ciraulo in 2015. The need for this service was discovered by surveying women who found that they were at a different stage of life as many of their current girlfriends and simply wanted to connect with women of similar interests, goals & hobbies.
