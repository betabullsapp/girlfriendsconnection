Single Women Looking for Other Single Women Girlfriends!

Girlfriendsconnection.com is an online service to bring together single women with like interests. You can find other single women in your geographic area to do fun (whatever you consider to be fun) things together.

About Girlfriendsconnection.com

There are so many online dating sites for singles to meet other singles to start a romantic relationship. But what about single women looking for new single girlfriends? Girlfriendsconnection.com is geared for women of any age looking for other women friends. Woman who are looking for a fun night out when their other female friends are with their husbands or partners. Are you tired of being a 3rd or 5th wheel to your friends who are part of a couple? Wouldn’t it be nice to have single girlfriends who want to go to the movies, vacation or out dancing?

Girlfriendsconnection.com is designed specifically to help you make new friends and to enhance your free time by forming relationships with similar people. To join Girlfriendsconnection.com, simply create a profile and provide some background info about yourself, as well as describe what kind of new friends you’re interested in meeting.

Meeting New Girlfriends

Once you’ve created your profile, you will be able to browse our network to find your girlfriend connections. You may select connections by any criteria you choose – age, location, hobbies, etc.

Sign up for Girlfriendsconnection.com today, meet new friends and plan some exciting GNO’s (girls night out)!

It’s time to join Girlfriendsconnection.com!

This service was founded by Jim Anan & Kim Ciraulo in 2013. The need was discovered by surveying women who found that, due to divorce or widowhood, they had no “single girlfriends” with similar hobbies or who were open to new hobbies or who just wanted to expand their social networks.
